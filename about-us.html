About Us

Logixal is an application software services company specializing in Digital Commerce and Digital Banking domains, We provide omni channel solutions for Retail, Luxury Goods. Financial Services, Media, Publishing, Telecom and Subscription Model based businesses.

Our customers, spread across the United States, Europe, Middle East, Africa and Asia, are supported from our Offices in India, UK and the USA. In the last 13 years, we have a 100% track record of successfully extending and implementing product software for various enterprises. Logixal is known to deliver superior total cost of ownership (TCO) in Re-platforming/Upgrades as well as Greenfield implementation projects.

Our Vision

Empower Digital Commerce and Digital Banking enterprises to embark on the next-gen transformations, enhancing digital experiences to boost revenues or cut costs.

At Logixal, our vision is to assist businesses to embrace cutting edge technology and strategies to provide exceptional Digital Experiences to their customers leading to increased revenue and decreased Total Cost of Ownership. We are committed to be a Digital Transformation Partner assisting customers to navigate the evolving landscape of Digital Commerce & Digital Banking, setting new industry standards and ensuring our clients' success in the dynamic digital landscape.

Our vision is to empower Digital Commerce and Digital Banking enterprises in their next-generation transformation journey, delivering:

  • Unparalleled Digital Experiences: We aspire to help businesses create digital experiences that captivate customers and drive top-line growth.
  • Reduced Total Cost of Ownership (TCO): Our solutions are designed to streamline operations and reduce the TCO, ensuring cost-efficiency,

Our Mission

Become a trusted partner of choice, through on-going delivery of high quality innovation an engineering solutions & services at optimized cost.

At Logixal, our unwavering mission is to be the go-to partner of choice. continually delivering superior innovation and engineering solutions and services, all while carefully optimizing costs. As trusted partner. our aim is to provide our customers with innovative and high-quality solutions tailored to their specific needs — be it developing cutting-edge software, designing efficient systems, or offering expert advice. We believe in forging long term relationships and are committed to our customefs success.

We are wholeheartedly dedicated to not only earning but also nurturing the trust we share with our valued clients.

  • Innovation: We drive progress through continuous innovation, providing creative solutions to meet evolving client needs and empower businesses in a dynamic market.
