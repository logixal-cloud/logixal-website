As Sls, we invest significantly in developing solutions around commercetools. The direct benefit for our customers is faster time to market. Some of our accelerators are:

B2C Storefront

Logixal's B2C frontend has been developed keeping in mind the needs and traits of typical B2C interactions. Designed to deliver a seamless and engaging user experience, it comes with out of the box features like easy navigation, product discovery, search, simplified checkout experience and other storefront features like wishlists, notifications etc. The B2C storefront comes with native support for:-

  • SSR, SSG, ISG, CSR for performance
  • SEO
  • Product recommendations through AI based Experience Engine.
  • PWA, Offline experience
  • Integration with Google Analytics
  • Storybook for building and documenting components
  • Business controlled page layouts
  • Optimized Web Vitals

B2B Storefront

Logixal's B2B frontend is tailored to the needs of a B2B customer. While it delivers a user experience similar to that of B2C commerce, it also delivers B2B specific features and functionlities like role-based dashboards, account management, quote management, order workflow, event based notifications etc. The B2C storefront comes with native support for:-

  • SSR, SSG, ISG, CSR for performance
  • SEO
  • Product recommendations through AI based Experience Engine.
  • PWA, Offline experience
  • Integration with Google Analytics
  • Storybook for building and documenting components
  • Business controlled page layouts
  • Optimized Web Vitals

BFF (AWS Lambda & SpringBoot micro-services)

Logixal's commercetools solutions include B2C and B2B Storefronts. optimizing user experiences for online shoppers and facilitating smooth 82B transactions. Their Backend for Frontend (BFF) comes in two flavors - AWS Lambda and SpringBoot microservices - enabling efficient communication between frontend and backend components. ensuring rapid response and scalability. These solutions empower businesses to deliver exceptional customer experiences, streamline processes, and drive growth.

AI/ML Experience Engine (Search, Recommendations and Trends)

Logixal's AI/ML Experience Engine enhances the customer journey with advanced Search, personalized Recommendations, and trend insights. It optimizes search results, boosts sales through tailored product suggestions, and helps businesses adapt to market trends. Integrated into commercetools solutions, it fosters customer loyalty and drives revenue growth.
